About the Shop

Brown Floral has been providing the Greater Salt Lake Valley with beautiful flowers since 1915. We have a rich history in this area that originated on the west side with shop owners, Leon and Leona Brown. The business stayed in the Brown family for decades but eventually sold. 100% Guarantee

Our commitment to you is 100% satisfaction on all your floral and gift purchases. If you are not completely satisfied, let us know by calling (215) 547-4550.

Delivery Policy

A local delivery fee of $10.00 will be added to each order in Fairless Hills, for each address. For deliveries outside this delivery area, this fee varies from $10.00 to $20.00.

We will do our best to accommodate deliveries at specific times of day, but we cannot guarantee it.

We are unable to make deliveries on Sundays. Weddings and funerals are an exception, since we can plan ahead and make accomodations on an as needed basis.

We will be happy to accept your international orders if you call our shop directly at 800-547-4551. We are unable to accept international orders over the Internet at this time.

Substitution Policy

Occasionally, substitutions may be necessary to create your bouquet due to the availability of certain flowers in various parts of the country. Care is taken to maintain the style, theme and color scheme of the arrangement, using flowers of equal value. Additionally, the substitution of certain keepsake items may be necessary due to increased demand, especially during major holidays. In single-flower arrangements, such as an all rose bouquet, or orchids, we will make every attempt to match the flower type, but may substitute with another color.
